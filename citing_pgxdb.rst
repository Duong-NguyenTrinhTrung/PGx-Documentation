Citing PGxDB
===============
If you use PGxDB in your work, please cite the following:

Duong Nguyen, T. T., Tanoli, Z., Hassan, S., Özcan, U. O., Caroli, J., Kooistra, A. J., ... & Hauser, A. S. (2025). 
PGxDB: an interactive web-platform for pharmacogenomics research. 
`Nucleic Acids Research, 53(D1), D1486–D1497 <https://academic.oup.com/nar/article/53/D1/D1486/7905303>`_.
